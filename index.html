About Us

           
               

NYC Yoga Studio is a community of inspired people joined together by our love of yoga. We are a place                     for all.                     When you join our family, you join a dedicated, conscious community of over 70 studios worldwide.                     See you soon in the hot room!                     Whether you are an absolute beginner or a more experienced student, we welcome all!                     We offer classes and group sessions to teach people specific body/mind self-improvement techniques.                     Our classes fall under the three categoriesmedidation, mindfullness and yoga!
